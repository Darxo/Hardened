### Main Changes ###

- Veteran Perks are disabled

## Balance
- Dodge effect is now depending on the adjacent empty tiles
- Duelist only works for one-handed weapons
- Formidable Approach only works for Two-Handed weapons
- Inspiring Presence no longer requires a banner, only affects brothers with less resolve. If multiple 'Inspiring Presence' perks are present on your brothers only one is active in each battle
- The skill 'Sprint' is no longer part of 'Pathfinder' and now it's part of 'Footwork'. 'Footwork' has been renamed to 'Escape Artist'

## Quality of Life
- Legendary Armor and Armor with an attachement that you un-equip are now automatically marked as to-be-repaired
- Night Effect no longer displays a Mini-Icon
- Double Grip no longer displays a Mini-Icon

### Requirements ###

- Reforged

### Known Issues: ###

- Enemies may spawn with perks that they can't use anymore because I didn't touch their spawning behaviors in that regard

### Compatibility ###

- Should be safe to remove from- and add to running games and should not corrupt anything upon removal
- Should be safe to remove from- and add to any existing savegames
